DENG HOTEL

Deng Hotel, a recent addition to New York City's boutique hotel scene, proudly stands in the vibrant Koreatown neighborhood, nestled between 32nd Street, Fifth Avenue, and Broadway. With proximity to iconic landmarks such as the Empire State Building, Macy's, the Fashion District, Penn Station, and Madison Square Garden, our guests enjoy unmatched convenience.

The Jacob K. Javits Convention Center is also just a stone's throw away, catering to business travelers. Our expansive, well-lit lobby offers complimentary Wi-Fi and houses a 24-hour business center with two iMacs. Our dedicated concierge team is at your service, eager to assist in planning sightseeing adventures and leisure activities, along with arranging convenient car services.

Dining and Culinary Delights

Indulge in a culinary experience at our elegant on-site restaurant, where we serve a diverse selection of international cuisine. Start your day with our delicious breakfast buffet featuring a wide variety of options. For a breathtaking view, visit our rooftop bar. We are also happy to accommodate special dietary requests, and private dining options are available for those seeking an intimate meal.

Entertainment and Nightlife

Enjoy a vibrant entertainment scene during your stay. We host live music and entertainment events for your enjoyment. Our hotel also features a casino or game room for guests looking for some excitement. If you're interested in learning a new skill, consider participating in cocktail-making classes or wine tastings . And when the sun sets, our nightclub or lounge comes to life with a DJ spinning your favorite tunes.

Leisure and Recreation

Our hotel offers a range of leisure and recreational amenities. Take a dip in our outdoor swimming pool or relax on the sun deck. Stay active with our fully-equipped fitness center, or unwind at our spa and wellness center. Our concierge service can help you plan local attractions and activities, and we offer bicycle and car rental for your convenience.

Our Options

OutDoor Garden Bar

After a day of exploring the city, head over to the Outdoor Garden Bar for a delightful experience amidst nature and breathtaking views of the city's skyline. This hidden oasis in the heart of the city offers daily happy hours, exclusive drink specials, and delectable appetizers to satisfy your cravings. Whether you prefer a cozy indoor nook or an open-air garden setting, you can take your pick, settle in, and order anything from craft beers to refreshing passionfruit cosmos or even a pitcher of zesty margaritas. Revel in the tranquil garden ambiance while enjoying stunning sunsets at this cherished local hotspot, just steps away from your NYC home base!
